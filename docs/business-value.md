# Business Value: PM Agentic Workflow Template Library

## Executive Summary

Program managers at technology companies spend an estimated 8–15 hours per week on 
information assembly tasks. This library automates six of the most time-consuming PM 
tasks, recovering an estimated $43,875 per year in strategic PM capacity per person.

## The Problem: Information Assembly Tax

| Task | Hours/Week |
|------|------------|
| Writing sprint status reports | 3–4 hours |
| Triaging new GitHub issues | 2–3 hours |
| Composing stakeholder updates | 2–4 hours |
| Scanning for at-risk items | 1–2 hours |
| Tracking PR cycle times | 1–2 hours |
| Checking docs for staleness | 1–2 hours |
| **TOTAL** | **10–15 hours** |

## ROI Analysis

- Average TPM salary: $130,000/year
- Effective hourly rate: $62.50/hour
- Hours saved per week: 10–15 hours
- Annual value recovered: $40,625 – $54,688

## What Recovered Time Enables

- More frequent 1:1s with engineering leads
- Deeper roadmap analysis and better prioritization
- Proactive stakeholder management
- Faster response to emerging risks

## Implementation Cost

- Setup time: approximately 4 hours total
- API costs: $0–$5/month (within free tier for most repos)
- Maintenance: approximately 15 minutes/month
- Payback period: less than one day of PM time

*Note: Hours saved are conservative estimates based on typical PM workflows. 
Actual savings vary by team size and repo activity.*
